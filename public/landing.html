If you travel, you've probably had this happen.

You know those friends that never seem to stop travelling? Chances are you're going to overlap at a destination sometime soon.

Only problem is, how will you know about it? The best you can hope for is a Facebook status once they've arrived. But what if you miss it?

Co:tripping notifies you of potential overlaps.

Co:tripping is the only app for inputting all your future travel plans, whether booked or just an idea.

It notifies you if you're going to overlap, or have a near miss, with one of your friends at a destination. It reminds you if you have friends who live there.

You can also view your friends' travel plans, creating more opportunities for joint trips.

Reconnect with old friends. Find new ones. Travel has never been so fun.

Going to a new city on a work trip and don't know anyone there? Co:tripping will search for friends of your friends at that destination.

Need a holiday but got no-one to go with? Look at your friends' upcoming trips and see if there are any you can join.
